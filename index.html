Why do I need more than one card?

You can maximize the rewards you earn across diverse
categories

Applying for a second Card is a great strategy to help you maximize
the rewards you can earn on your spending. If you earn bonus Membership
Rewards® points or cash back in only one or two categories, look for a
second Card where you can earn rewards for purchases in different categories.

There's a way to maximize spending for many lifestyles.

Some benefits are even better together

Even if you are earning a lot of points or cash back in a spending category,
you may be missing out on benefits relevant to that category if you only
have one card.
If you travel frequently, look for a second travel card that has unique
benefits—you can expand your travel benefits by having multiple Cards.
